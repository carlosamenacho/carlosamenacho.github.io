Photography

A gilmpse to the many photos I have taken over time. I now shoot on a Sony A7RIII, however some of my older photos were taken with a DSLR(Canon T7i). Personally, I mostly enjoy scenic urban photography, which reflects on my work. However, I have also grown skillful at portrait, experimental, wedding, and event photography.

Skills and platforms most profiectent.

From a young age, film and photography were always a part of my life. In the years I have been working with videos and photography I have become most comfortable with the Adobe suite. For all purposes of editing, whether that be audio, video, or stills. I've gained quite a bit of knowledge and understanding on how each play and work together to create a more interesting and professional completed project.

As seen in many of my videos, the different cameras I have used has made me comfortable with a wide range of cameras. Though I began small, using just a phone camera to start, I became proficient with a DSLR and a Mirrorless. A Canon T7i was my starter camera for videos and photos. I would later use Lumix G7 and finally the Sony A7Riii. However in my studies, I was able to learn to use larger camcorders as well.

All in all, I have become well rounded in my work, learning how to use tools for better lighting, audio, visuals, and even animations so that the end product is one that surpasses expectations. I look forward to producing challenging projects so that I can go above and beyond. Creating media is my passion.
